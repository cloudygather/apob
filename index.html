What’s
Shared-space?

"Shared-space" is the co-banking brand of Newnew, which has been newly established. We welcome you to join us in any type of space, including bookstores, restaurants, coffee shops, or guest hotels, among others.

As long as you share the space of your store with us, we will offer equipment and an educational plan to accompany you. Ultimately, your brand will be easily transformed into a shared space. Let's "share space and bank together."
